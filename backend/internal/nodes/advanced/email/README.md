# Advanced Email Nodes

This directory contains advanced email nodes for the Citadel Agent workflow engine.

## Overview
Advanced email nodes provide sophisticated email automation including:
- Templated email generation
- Bulk email processing
- Email tracking and analytics
- Personalized content delivery
- Multi-channel delivery

## Available Nodes
- `email_template_processor_node.go` - Processes email templates with dynamic content
- `bulk_email_sender_node.go` - Handles bulk email sending operations
- `email_analytics_node.go` - Tracks email performance metrics
- `personalized_email_node.go` - Generates personalized email content

## Usage
These nodes are designed for complex email marketing and communication workflows.

## Requirements
- Email service provider credentials
- Valid email templates
- Recipient data lists

## Security & Permissions
- Requires email service access permissions
- May require access to recipient data
