# Intermediate Email Nodes

This directory contains intermediate email nodes for the Citadel Agent workflow engine.

## Overview
Intermediate email nodes provide moderately advanced email operations including:
- Dynamic email templating
- Multi-channel email delivery
- Email engagement tracking
- Personalization engines
- Campaign management

## Available Nodes
- `dynamic_template_node.go` - Creates emails from dynamic templates
- `multi_channel_sender_node.go` - Sends emails via multiple email providers
- `engagement_tracker_node.go` - Tracks email opens, clicks, and engagement
- `personalization_engine_node.go` - Personalizes email content
- `campaign_manager_node.go` - Manages email campaign workflows

## Usage
These nodes are designed for intermediate email workflows with enhanced personalization.

## Requirements
- Email template management system
- Multiple email service provider credentials
- Tracking metrics systems
- Personalization data sources

## Security & Permissions
- Requires multi-provider email access permissions
- Campaign management privileges
